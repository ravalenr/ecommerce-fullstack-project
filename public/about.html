Our Story

Multi Store Eletro was founded with a simple mission: to provide quality electronics and appliances at fair prices while delivering exceptional customer service. Based in Sao Paulo, we have been serving our community with dedication and integrity.

Our Mission

To market quality products at fair prices, exceeding customer expectations with specialized and personalized service.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the best Electronics and Appliances store, establishing ourselves in the market as a reference in trust, quality, and excellence.
